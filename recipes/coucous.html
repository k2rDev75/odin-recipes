Couscous

Description

A flavorful chicken stew that uses aromatic spices and nice vegetables served over whole-grain couscous. This recipe was actually a real chicken contest winner! Vegetables and even meat can vary according to taste, but I find squash, carrots, bell peppers, and lamb work well in this recipe

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
